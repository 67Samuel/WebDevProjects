The Best Movies According to Samuel

My top 3 movies of all time

Along With The Gods: The Two Worlds

Isa introduced this to me - I like it because it is thought provoking, doesn't stay in my mind for very long, and very well done in terms of acting and cinematography.

Kimi no Na wa

Beautiful animation. I cried.

Pay It Forward

Great movie with a good lesson and the innocence of a child, showing what the world can and should be.
